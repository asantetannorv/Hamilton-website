Welcome to My Hamilton Themed Website

Hamilton is a Broadway musical composed by Lin-Manuel Miranda that was first performed in 2015. It has become one of my favorite musicals to watch and has a really good soundtrack that goes viral every year, especially on social media apps like TikTok where people reuse songs from the show in trends. The plot of Hamilton tells the story of Alexander Hamilton's life, from when he was sent to New York from the Caribbean at age 19 to when he died in a duel. The musical mixes history with modern music styles like rap and hip-hop, which makes it different from traditional Broadway shows and more interesting to watch. Throughout the musical, many historical figures are portrayed. He meets his arch-nemesis, Aaron Burr, who eventually ends his life in the famous duel. He also becomes friends with John Laurens, Marquis de Lafayette, and Hercules Mulligan, and together they fight in the American Revolutionary War. During this time, Hamilton marries Eliza Schuyler Hamilton and forms a close relationship with her sister, Angelica Schuyler. He later becomes the right-hand man to George Washington, who trusts him during the war and in politics. Later in his life, after building a name for himself in government, Hamilton often argues with Thomas Jefferson and James Madison over political issues. The musical shows many of his successes, but also his mistakes, including personal scandals and the choices that led to his downfall. Overall, Hamilton is not only entertaining but also educational, because it teaches history in a way that is easier to understand and more engaging than reading from a textbook.
